Overview

FloodGuard predicts floods using sensor and weather data, issues timely alerts to users, shows safe or unaffected roadways on a map, and can share live locations with rescue teams.

  • Mobile-friendly alerts (native notifications + in-page helpers)
  • Live location sharing to rescue dashboard
